Technical Excellence

Key Features of Our Generator Sets

We focus on reliability, efficiency, and ease of maintenance.

**Genuine Components:** We exclusively use engines from Perkins, Volvo Penta, and Doosan, coupled with Stamford or Leroy-Somer alternators.

**Efficiency:** All engines are designed for optimal fuel efficiency and meet international emission standards.

**Deep Sea Controllers:** Standardized use of Deep Sea Electronics (DSE) control modules for reliable monitoring and protection.

**Remote Monitoring:** Optional GSM/GPRS modules for remote monitoring and control via mobile application.

**Custom Fabrication:** Heavy-duty, weather-resistant canopies with advanced sound-absorbing materials.

**Low Noise:** Engineered to achieve noise levels as low as 65 dB at 7 meters, suitable for sensitive environments.

**Turnkey Solutions:** Full service from site preparation, installation, commissioning, to load bank testing.

**24/7 Support:** Dedicated emergency response team and comprehensive service contracts available.
