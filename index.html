Technical Excellence

Key Features of Our Generator Sets

We focus on reliability, efficiency, and ease of maintenance.

Premium Engine & Alternator

We exclusively use **Perkins, Volvo Penta, and Doosan** engines, coupled with **Stamford or Leroy-Somer** alternators for guaranteed performance and global support.

  • Optimal fuel efficiency and low emissions.
  • Genuine OEM components for maximum lifespan.

Advanced Control & Monitoring

Standardized use of **Deep Sea Electronics (DSE)** control modules for reliable monitoring, protection, and user-friendly operation.

  • Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) compatibility.
  • Optional remote monitoring via mobile app.

Superior Soundproof Canopy

Heavy-duty, weather-resistant canopies with advanced sound-absorbing materials, engineered for sensitive environments.

  • Noise levels as low as 65 dB at 7 meters.
  • Excellent ventilation for optimal performance.

Turnkey Installation & 24/7 Service

Full service from site preparation, installation, commissioning, to load bank testing, backed by a dedicated emergency team.

  • Certified engineering team for all work.
  • Guaranteed genuine spare parts and fast response.
